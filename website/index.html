Travails of Æternum

Now In Development!

Travails of Æternum is an upcoming retro SNES-style JRPG in which ...

  • you, a demon-born champion, recruit and liberate other created beings
  • use a pact-exchange combat system that lets you steal, swap, and combine your makers' powers
  • turn the unholy pantheon against itself while choosing between destruction, redemption, or ascension
